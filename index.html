About the Gym

A No-Frills Corner To Get In Real Fighting Shape

619 Boxing Gym is a brand-new, community-focused boxing gym in San Diego's south side offering professional boxing training, strength & conditioning, and personal training for kids and adults of all skill levels.

Reviewers praise the extremely knowledgeable, experienced, and welcoming coaching staff, calling it a very professional boxing gym with affordable prices and flexible hours. The gym offers a free first session and prides itself on a supportive, high-energy community atmosphere for beginners and competitive boxers alike.

Expect a clean, modern, garage-style space and hands-on coaching, no-frills training built around the neighborhood.

Questions

Good To Know

Do I need boxing experience to start?

No. 619 Boxing Gym offers classes for beginners through advanced boxers, so you can start at your own level and progress from there.

Is there a class for kids?

Yes. Kids boxing classes run on weekdays from 4-5pm.

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes, the gym offers a free first session so you can see the space, meet the coaches, and try a class before deciding.

What kind of training is offered besides boxing?

Alongside boxing training and classes, the gym offers strength & conditioning and personal training for a well-rounded program.

What are the gym's hours?

Monday through Friday, 8am, 8pm, and Saturday 8am, 2pm.
